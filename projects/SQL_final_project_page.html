Exploratory Analysis of Book Bans Across the USA

Tool — SQL/Looker Studio
Year — 2025

Overview

This project was an exploratory study on book bans throughout the US. All of the data analysis was conducted in SQL with the help of Looker Studio to make interactive and visually appealing graphics.

What I found

We knew that the rate of book bans had been rising in recent years and were curious to see what other trends followed. We noticed a lot of political trends in our data, for example, the increase in book bans likely due to the sentiment towards banned books from the Trump administration. There was a correlation with the top states in book bans and voting Republican in the 2024 election.

Process

After heavily cleaning the data to match the date format, only include the groups we were interested in and fix other small errors, I merged the book ban data with the other data sources in separate SQL tables. After this, I manually small groupings for age, race, etc. Once the data had been prepared, I did small calculations to find diversity in a states by age, race, and political measures. These findings were visually displayed through Looker Studio in the form of bar charts, heat maps, and combination graphics.
